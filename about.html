Industrial PhD Candidate at KTH Computer and Software System, RISE SICS, and Senior Researcher at Ericsson Research under the supervision of Seif Haridi, Šarūnas Girdzijauskas, Daniel Gillblad and Rickard Cöster.

My research interest includes deployment and automation of Machine Intelligence functions in distributed Radio Access Network environments.

The work is partially supported by the Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems and Software Program (WASP) funded by the Knut and Alice Wallenberg Foundation.
